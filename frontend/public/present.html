What makes us different

The only Glass Box

  • Every decision logged in plain English — auditable from day one, not bolted on. Built to Colorado SB21-169, NAIC & NY DFS.
  • Plug-and-play — no front-end rebuild. API-first: drops straight into the carrier's existing app, inside their own Microsoft tenant. No new vendor, no rip-and-replace.
  • Human-in-the-loop by design — AI settles the routine, escalates the rest. Never an opaque autopilot.
How precise

Grounded · scored · cross-checked

  • RAG with citations — the Policy agent answers from the actual policy document, not a guess. No hallucination.
  • Confidence-scored every time — ≥90% auto-settle, 60–90% to an adjuster, <60% human escalation.
  • Five agents cross-validate — extraction → coverage → fraud & fact checks → adjudication, each traceable to evidence.
How scalable

Flat cost at 100M+ policies

  • Indexed store + Azure AI Search — indexed seeks, not generative scans: latency & token cost stay flat as data grows.
  • Serverless + parallel agents — elastic by default; thousands of claims process at once.
  • Enterprise Microsoft backbone — Dataverse, Functions & Power Automate scale to carrier volume.
